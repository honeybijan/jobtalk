Literature on Sample Complexity of Mixtures

  • Mixtures of Gaussians: $\Omega(exp(k))$
    • Moitra and Valiant. "Settling the polynomial learnability of mixtures of gaussians." (FOCS 2010).
  • Mixtures of Discrete Variables: $\Omega(exp(k))$
    • Rabani, Schulman, and Swamy. "Learning mixtures of arbitrary distributions over large discrete domains." (ITCS 2014).
  • Mixture of Products: $O(exp(k^2 \log(k))) \rightarrow O(exp(k \log(k)))$
    • Mazaheri et. al. "Source Identification for Mixtures of Product Distributions." (COLT 2021).
    • Mazaheri et. al. "Identification of Mixtures of Discrete Product Distributions in Near-Optimal Sample and Time Complexity" (Hopefully COLT 2024)
